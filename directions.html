From San Francisco and the Bay Area:
1. Follow US-101 North to Petaluma.
2. Take the East Washington Street Exit, cross over the freeway, and head west.
3. Continue for about 25 miles to Bodega Bay.
4. Turn left on Eastshore Road. Terrapin Creek will be on your immediate right in the Blue Whale Center.

From Santa Rosa and the East:
1. Follow Highway 12 (Bodega Highway) from Santa Rosa through Sebastapol and Bodega to its end at the Coast Highway One.
2. Turn right and go about five miles north to the town of Bodega Bay.
3. Turn left on Eastshore Road. Terrapin Creek will be on your immediate right in the Blue Whale Center.

From the North:
1. Take Highway One south from Mendocino or River Road west from US-101 and Guerneville, to Jenner.
2. Proceed south on Coast Highway One ten miles to Bodega Bay.
3. Turn right on Eastshore Road. Terrapin Creek will be on your immediate right in the Blue Whale Center.
